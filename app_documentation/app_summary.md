# MyJobMatchr: Your Essential Job Search Companion

## Purpose
MyJobMatchr is a web application designed to make job searching **faster, smarter, and personalized**. It helps candidates focus only on opportunities that truly match their profile, saving time and increasing application impact.

—

## Key Features
* **Intelligent Job Aggregation:** Collects job postings from multiple platforms, removes duplicates, and organizes them for easy access.
* **Smart Matching:** Matches candidate CVs and preferences (role, industry, location, salary, etc.) with the most relevant jobs.
* **Filtering & Prioritization:** Ranks jobs by relevance and freshness, letting candidates focus on high-value opportunities first.
* **Application Enhancement:** Generates personalized cover letters, suggests CV improvements, and tracks applications and recruiter responses.

—

## Core Benefits
* **Time-saving:** Avoid irrelevant or duplicate postings.
* **High relevance:** Curated job list tailored to each candidate.
* **Full support:** From profile optimization to application tracking.
* **User-friendly:** Intuitive, web-based interface with real-time updates.

---
## Vision
To become the essential companion for job seekers, transforming a stressful, inefficient process into a **proactive, productive, and personalized experience**.
